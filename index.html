Hello There

We Are Midcoast CoWork

We are a coworking space located in Midcoast Maine. We work together, while working for different companies and organizations. Our members work for universities, non-profits, banks, and tech companies. We have a strong sense of community and cooperation, and we are always happy to welcome new people.

Our Space

Overlooking Downtown Damariscotta

We are located on the second floor of the historic and well-cared for Damariscotta Center building. With high tin ceilings and tall windows overlooking downtown Damariscotta, our space offers dedicated desks as well as shared space at either of our two large conference tables.
